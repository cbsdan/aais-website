Programs Offered

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten Program is designed to ignite curiosity and lay the foundation for lifelong learning. We provide a nurturing and engaging environment where children begin their educational journey with hands-on experiences and interactive lessons. Through play, exploration, and guided learning, children develop important social, cognitive, and motor skills.

Elementary

Our Elementary Program is designed to foster a love for learning in a nurturing environment. Students in Kindergarten through Grade 6 are introduced to foundational subjects that build essential skills for academic success and personal growth.

Junior High

Our High School Program provides students with a more comprehensive and structured approach to their education. In this phase, students begin to specialize in subjects of interest, preparing them for the academic demands of Senior High School and future careers.

Senior High

Our Senior High School Program offers a diverse range of specialized tracks designed to prepare students for their next steps — whether it be higher education or a successful career in their chosen field.

School Activities

Monthly Celebrations

Every month, we come together to celebrate important events, cultural holidays, and awareness campaigns that help broaden our understanding of the world. Whether it's a national holiday, a cultural festival, or an environmental day, our monthly celebrations are a fun and educational way to honor what makes our community unique.

Sports Activities

Sports at our school are more than just games—they’re an opportunity to learn important life skills. Our sports program offers students the chance to participate in a variety of activities, from team sports like basketball and soccer to individual events like track and field. Whether you’re competing in a school competition or simply enjoying physical activity, sports are an essential part of student life.

Drills and Preparedness

At our school, we also take safety seriously. Drills are regular practices to ensure that all students know exactly what to do in case of emergencies like fire, earthquakes, or other safety situations. These drills are designed to teach students how to stay calm, follow instructions, and be prepared for any emergency that may arise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Army's Angels Integrated School provides a range of educational programs from early childhood to secondary education. The curriculum is designed to offer a holistic approach, incorporating both academic subjects and character development. Specialized programs in arts, sports, and leadership are also available to cater to diverse student interests.

The school supports student development through personalized learning plans, extracurricular activities, and a focus on both academic and personal growth. Teachers provide individualized attention and mentorship to help students achieve their potential. The school also emphasizes values education and community involvement to build well-rounded individuals.

Admission to Army's Angels Integrated School typically requires the completion of an application form, submission of previous school records, and an entrance assessment. Prospective students and parents may also need to attend an interview or orientation session. The school seeks students who demonstrate academic potential, enthusiasm for learning, and alignment with the school's values.
